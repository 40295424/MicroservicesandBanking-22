FAQ's

 

What happens if I accidentally deleted a customer?

You are able to quickly retrieve the information you just deleted by clicking on the retrieve button.

 

Where can I view the database of customer transactions?

There is a customer database button on the dashboard by clicking it you should be able to see the database which holds all the customers information.

 

What support is available?

By clicking the support button you are able to see the queries asked previously, the queries that are popular and any of us who can help you with any queries about the app.

 

How to search for a customer?

You can search for a customer by clicking on the quick search button in the dashboard you will need the Customer ID to search for them.
